Price

There are no membership fees and no hidden costs. Simply hire me for the day or half day and pay for my time plus any expenses incurred on your behalf.

Use services on a regular basis or just as a one-off. And make the most of a free 1 hour business consultation for all new clients!

You will have benefit of having your own Personal Assistant, without the hassle involved with actually employing some-one. I work on an entirely freelance basis and you only pay me for the work I do – recruitment costs, holiday pay, sickness pay, benefits, pensions, are all a thing of the past!

Please note, these fees are a guide, every client is different.

I can either work on an hourly, half day or full day basis, you choose which hours you need me to work and I can be flexible to fit around your requirements.

All rates will be calculated on an individual basis, depending on your personal requirements, regularity of bookings and the type of work you would like me to carry out. For example I would not charge the same fee for marketing as for filing.

I would require 48 hours’ notice to cancel any booked services or time or 30 days’ notice to cancel an ongoing arrangement.

My ‘on-site’ fees are based on either a full or half day starting at £25 per hour plus expenses and VAT. We can discuss all of this during your business consultation and provide a full quotation based on how I can best serve your needs.
